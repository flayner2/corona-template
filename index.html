Symptoms of Coronavirus

Those who are infected with COVID-19 may have little to no symptoms. You may not know you have symptoms of COVID-19 because they are similar to a cold or flu.

Symptoms may take up to 14 days to appear after exposure to COVID-19. This is the longest known infectious period for this disease. We are currently investigating if the virus can be trasmitted to others if someone is not showing symptoms. While experts believe that it is possible, it is considered less common.

Symptoms have included:

  • Fever
  • Headache
  • Runny Nose
  • Throat Pain
  • Shaking Chills
  • Dypsnoea

In severe cases, infection can lead to death.
